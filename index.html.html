About me

Quality is not a department.
It's a mindset.

I started on the foundry floor — inspecting every core, every melt, every pour at an IATF 16949 automotive casting plant supplying BorgWarner, Renault, and Daimler. I learned that quality is built in, not inspected out.

After three years growing from intern to shift team leader at Greentech Industries India, I came to Germany to understand how quality works at the next level. At Erwin Junker Maschinenfabrik, I didn't just study digitalization — I built it. A full Smart QMS prototype, from blank canvas to real production validation.

My goal is to work where precision manufacturing meets digital transformation — helping companies move from paper-based quality to intelligent, traceable, audit-ready systems. Eventually, I want to build quality management tools that small manufacturers can actually afford and use.
